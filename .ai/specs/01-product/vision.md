# Product Vision

One sentence that states what the product is, who it's for, and why it exists.

> **For** [target customer]
> **Who** [need/problem]
> **The** [product name]
> **Is a** [product category]
> **That** [key benefit / value proposition]
> **Unlike** [competitor/alternative]
> **Our product** [key differentiator]

## Long-term Vision

2-3 paragraphs describing the future state of the product in 3-5 years.
